#Group 

A group typically seen as an insane group of pyromaniacs, they were much more organised and conniving then originally thought.

## Origins
It was recently revealed that the originator of the cult was none other than past king of the [[Tresden Empire]], king Norvilles, royal advisor Brenger Vlam. After being radicalised by a rebellion rooted in distaste for the King and becoming enthralled by the power of soul bound eternal flame, Brenger killed Norville and fled. After learning to harness the power of the eternal flame and becoming a herald for Imix the ancient primordial of Fire he began gathering followers.

People who were cast out and held resentment towards the system, people who craved power and people who could be manipulated especially. He granted these people a taste of the power of eternal flame giving them a link to the plane of fire. As the cult grew a select group of highly loyal people were promoted to high priests to serve as the public facing leaders of the cult.

At first the cults activities were few and far between, long rituals around a burning effigy of Imix and the occasional kidnapping to sacrifice but as the cults number and powers grew over centuries so did their confidence. They scaled up the ritual murders and began burning buildings trapping everyone inside, even on occasion sending out a highly skilled death squad to burn small towns and their people.

## Beliefs
The core belief of the cult is that the world as we know it is impure and is too far gone to be fixed by normal means. They believe the only cure is the purifying eternal flame, similar to the smelting of raw metal ores, the heat of the flame burns away the superficial and leaves only the pure, strong and reliable behind, to be moulded and formed in their vision.

To achieve this the cult worships Imix, the all-consuming fire and lord of the elemental plan of fire, who gives them their abilities and with every sacrifice their fires burn brighter. Their minds are twisted by visions of the plane of fire forced upon them by Imix and this drives many cultists to loose parts of their sanity.
