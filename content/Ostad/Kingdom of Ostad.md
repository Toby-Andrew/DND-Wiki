## About
Ostad is a highly respected Kingdom on the continent, Being an empire that prides itself on its scientific research and advancements for the world.

A large focus for Ostad is military research which it sells off to other empires as the primary source of income for the kingdom, other forms of research such as agriculture and general life improvement technologies are still a focus but have taken a backseat to military to pull in funding for these other research initiatives.

Ostad is very protective about their knowledge and all discoveries or inventions and views them as their property. it is very rare that a discovery is published publicly, instead it published within the Kingdoms great library requiring special permissions to access. Any other kingdom/empire can request to read or use some knowledge but the process is very lengthy and strict, and with a fee of course. Even when granted they are monitored and must be transparent with their use completely.


## Location
Ostad is located on the north east coast of the continents great lake, and directly borders the [[Tresden Empire]] on the south border of Ostad.

Ostads Layout is similar to a web, with the Capital in the centre and 4 cities surrounding it. Every city has a road directly to the Capital and a road that circles the capital connecting each city with its neighbours.

### Biomes
Most of Ostad is a green and grassy land, however they have other diverse land types across the kingdom.
#### The Arid Mountains
To the Southeast is a large mountain range with many high peaks. The environment is very rocky, warm and dry creating a harsh environment, a result of [[Tresden Empire]]s desert peaking over the border, thus not much wildlife resides there permanently with the exception of specialised beasts that burrow the mountains, as the surface lacks both water or enough plant life to sustain most surface dwelling creatures. 

Two of the mountains were hollowed out in a large mining operation leading to the creation of the mining city [[Crepolis]].
#### The Great Forest
In Contrast to the arid mountains, in the northeast is a lush and dense forest teeming with life. The trees here grow spectacularly large and create a dense oxygen rich environment. These trees were the subject of a expansive research initiative to discover how they were able to grow so large being so far from a visible water source. Eventually it was discovered their roots delve so deep they are able to feed off the mineral rich subterranean water tunnels that feed the great lake from the ocean.

A city was developed here amidst the trees ranging from on the surface to the forest to up in the trees themselves named [[Troiphia]].

#### The lush Wetlands
The northwest of Ostad has a dense and moist environment perfect for cultivating crops. This biome was not originally what it was now, it was first a simple plain grassland. The decision was made to terraform the land to create an extremely fertile farmland to feed the growing population of Ostad. The method used to create this extremely arable is held tightly under wraps in Ostads knowledge library, however they did it the crops here grow at many times the speed they would in even the most fertile land elsewhere.

An agriculture and farming city was built on this land named [[Alediff]].

## Relations
Being a largely academic kingdom a lot of the manufacturing for their discoveries are outsourced to other empires/kingdoms. Ostad would pay for the materials from other kingdoms as well as their manufacturing expertise, sending an envoy to ensure the construction fits their design while still keeping its inner workings and development under wraps.

### Tresden
A popular import was the mineral [[Uthalt]] from the [[Tresden Empire]]. Because of its properties it was used widely by Ostad for its ability to revolutionise machinery and speed up research tenfold. Naturally, overtime Ostad developed a reliance on [[Uthalt]], and as a result, they relied on the [[Tresden Empire]] as well. In an attempt to lessen this reliance and lower import costs on the very expensive ore, Ostad began a mining initiative of their own in the [[#The Arid Mountains]] to the south east. 

The mines were built deep into the mountains even hollowing out 2 completely, essentially creating a city within them, solely for mining. [[Crepolis]] they called it. The main goal was, of course, to strike [[Uthalt]], however a healthy side effect was the kingdoms own source for other metals and precious minerals.

However after years of mining they were unable to find [[Uthalt]] in their land, although the mine wasn't a complete failure, having its own source of metals etc lowered its other import costs allowing for the import of [[Uthalt]] to continue with less of a blow financially.

When the time came, earlier than ever projected, when [[Uthalt]] mining slowed and eventually ran dry within [[Tresden Empire]]. Ostad had to adapt quickly, the existing [[Uthalt]] became infinitely more precious. Inventions and machinery being produced that used the wonder ore had to stop to reserve what little was left for important use only.

Despite this, Ostad continued to thrive with very little to no downtime, even without the [[Uthalt]], while all other nations took years to readapt, [[Tresden Empire]] especially. 

Since they no longer produced anything of use to Ostad, Tresden lost its largest Source of income, but as a show of good faith and respect Ostad granted [[Tresden Empire]]s Kings court access to much of their knowledge for a time, before king Norville was killed centuries ago. After which the deal was never restated.
