## About
Tresden Was a once great empire with a high level of influence on the continent being the sole source of [[Uthalt]]. However the [[Uthalt]] mines ran dry a few centuries ago, and Tresden was unable to bounce back, resorting to doing only basic trading of resources like food and lumber etc.
Before [[The Great Burn]], Tresden was coasting on left over finances from the [[Uthalt]] trading era and was considered a very poor Country.

Currently Tresden is a barren land most of the population fled to seek asylum with other empires/kingdoms. After [[The Great Burn]], even though the [[Eternal Flame Cult]] was stopped by a group of heroes and the heat stopped climbing, it never dropped. The damage to the infrastructure was too expensive to repair, and on top of this with no royal family or any other leadership the empire was disbanded. 

Due to its current climate environment as well as the lack of desirable natural resources, no other country has attempted to make claim to the land.

### Biomes
Tresden was comprised of two biomes that dominate the entire area.

#### The Vast Desert
To the east of Tresden is an expansive desert that took up roughly a third of the countries area before [[The Great Burn]], after which it took up an estimated 80%. It is a mostly flat desert with sparse plant life and has many rocky outcrops scattered throughout. Despite its conditions many life forms live in this desert, including many dangerous beasts such as, desert trolls, Sandwyrms and the mysterious sand silhouettes, among many more.

Buried beneath the desert are many ancient and uncovered ruins of a long lost civilisation that existed before Tresdens empire was ever formed, not much is known about the people of this civilisation other than they seemed to have a strong connection to magic that last until even after they vanished.

The most important thing hidden within these desert lands however, is [[Uthalt]]. The ore was discovered centuries ago by complete accident during an archaeological dig deep in the desert where its unique properties were first observed. Since then as far as it is known this desert is the only place where [[Uthalt]] could be mined. A small City named [[Senzad]] was developed on the outskirts of the desert where the temperature is more bearable to serve as a headquarters for the mining operation before the ore ran dry.

#### The pleasant Plains
To the west of Tresden is a lovely grass land with plentiful trees and other flora. this area was greatly reduced after [[The Great Burn]]. The plains were a temperate grasslands created by the mix of the heat from the desert and the water fed soil from the great lake through rivers and a smaller sub-lake. the plains also has many forests which gives plenty of lumber for the cities and towns.

Within the plains are two of the three major cities, [[Garilant]], the capital located between two rivers on the coast of the great lake, allowing access for a port. The other is [[Brenway]], the magic academy city. Its located to the east of [[Garilant]] near the edge of the desert, which allows them access to a cave within the desert that they use a safe space to practice certain sects of magic for their students.

## Relations
In its prime Tresden was perhaps the most important Empire on the continent, being the only source of the one of most precious resources ever known, [[Uthalt]]. Every nation traded with Tresden to acquire the miracle ore making Tresden a verifiable fortune. After the mines ran dry trade with other nations followed suit. Of course basic trade for food or materials continued but that was not enough to sustain the empire long term.

### Ostad
Perhaps the largest Importer of [[Uthalt]], The [[Kingdom of Ostad]] and Tresden had a close trade relationship for a number of years. After a time, it became clear Ostad wanted to lessen their reliance on Tresden. This became most evident to Tresden once Ostad began the construction of their own mine so close to the border to try and strike [[Uthalt]] themselves. This Angered King Norville, the king of Tresden at the time. King Norville kept his disdain for their attempted undermining to himself and his advisor Brenger.

Ostads mine never hit [[Uthalt]] by all reports and their trade continued, however once the Tresdens mines started to lessen output far earlier than Norvilles experts projected it was cut off completely. No more trade with Ostad was a huge blow to Tresdens finances and was a large factor in Tresden becoming the poorest country on the Continent.

After Tresden had no more [[Uthalt]] to trade, The [[Kingdom of Ostad]] granted King Norville and his court access to their knowledge libraries as a show of good faith, this was put to great use by Brenger and others but Norville himself viewed this act as a façade to maintain Ostads image and always secretly believed that Ostads mine was not a failure at all.

After King Norvilles Death this deal was never restated and trade between the two kingdoms halted completely.
