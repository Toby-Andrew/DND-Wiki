Garilant is the capital of the [[Tresden Empire]] located in the south west of the country. Garilant is by far the largest of the three cities in Tresden by a considerable margin. Most businesses and roughly eighty percent of the population lives here.

#### Infrastructure
The city was built near the south west coast, around a large river flowing in from the great lake a majority of the city is on the south coast of this river with only the richest neighbourhoods and businesses such as banks and private schools to the north along with the castle of the king. The entire city is surrounded by a sizable wall with large gates at key points along it. 

At the mouth of the river is an extremely extensive port, built to accommodate the largest trade ships as was needed back when [[Tresden Empire]] had its expansive [[Uthalt]] exporting up and running, even then its struggled to meet the demand.

A large portion of the city near the port was dedicated to [[Uthalt]] refineries making it the cities biggest industry in its time. After Uthalt exporting was halted these refineries were left empty for decades and left for ruin.

#### Architecture
Because of the cities once vast wealth, the capitals buildings are built extremely well and by some of the best architects from around the continent. Most public buildings such as schools, libraries, halls, temples etc are built from the finest stone with tasteful trimmings carved in. The roads were also extremely well built and taken care of to support the almost constant carriage and foot traffic. Having plenty of places for rest and recreation was also important to the city with many public parks and community centres spaced throughout the city. Much like most things these all began to slowly fall into disrepair as the [[Uthalt]] money dried up.

#### [[The Great Burn]]
During the [[Eternal Flame Cult]]s attack the entire city was pelted with blasts of fire burning down many buildings, the protective wall was blown open in many places allowing for cultists and creatures of fire to flood in many of which also came from within the castle, a pronged attack from within and outside the city. The river itself was not safe from this either, it began boiling and was even used to create fire infused water elementals to aid the attack, killing any who attempted fleeing by boat. After the attack was defeated, most of the infrastructure was not salvageable and what was left of the population fled to neighbouring countries.

The city was left abandoned and in scorched disrepair and that is how it has been for the past six months unlikely to be restored any time soon.
