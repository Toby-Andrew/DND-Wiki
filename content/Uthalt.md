The miracle metal of the [[Tresden Empire]].

## About
Discovered during an archeologic dig in the Desert of the [[Tresden Empire]] centuries ago, Uthalt became perhaps the most important resource in the continent, perhaps the world. Once stumbled upon its strange properties were discovered and a mining operation was immediately set up in the nearby city of [[Senzad]] and research on the ore began. 

It took months just to crack the refining process, making it ready for practical use. Once enough was refined the first batch was released to the most respected laboratories and colleges in the country to explore potential uses and reactions with other materials. The first uses of the metal where used to create instruments within these institutes, speeding up research on Uthalt exponentially as well as the mining process using tools made from Uthalt itself.

Very quickly Uthalt grew to high demand and became Tresdens primary export. Such high demand that a number of other countries began initiatives to find Uthalt in their own lands, all unsuccessful. A notable attempt was the [[Kingdom of Ostad]] who went as far as to hollow out two mountains on the border of the Tresden desert close to where Uthalt is mined, however it seemed not close enough. This mine became known as [[Crepolis]]. 

After many prosperous years, the ore quickly grew rarer, quicker than projections expected. This caught Tresden by surprise and when the ore eventually became too few and far between to realistically sustain the mining operation and it had to be shut down, the country took a huge hit. Because projections had the ore continuing expected capacity for many more years, steps to prepare were not taken and a large enough store was not built up.

All Uthalt tools for mining and smelting and were repurposed to try and conserve what was left. In the present Uthalt has become a sought after and rare resource outside of prebuilt infrastructure and machines.

## Properties
Uthalt is a metal with properties not seen in any other material, making it extremely valuable. It is capable of storing and channelling magic extremely efficiently, able to retain it seemingly indefinitely without any drop in potency and even small amounts of it can absorb an extreme quantity of magical energy. Its use as components in many magical constructions or items allows these magical objects to do things not possible in any other form.

To transform the raw ore into a more pure state requires a very difficult and precise smelting and curing method. The levels of heat required to melt Uthalt are very hard to achieve and usually require a special type of blast furnace. Although with enough knowledge it is possible to do it using magical means, however uthalts properties mean that refining this way leads to the metal absorbing the magic used to create the fire that smelts it and can result in unintended effects. As such it is rarely refined this way.

Once smelted Uthalt must be poured into a mould while still in liquid form as once hardened in its pure state it is almost impossible to reshape without other specialised Uthalt tools and/or high level advanced magic.
